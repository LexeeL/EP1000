Making a Music Box

Navigation:

This page explains the process of me designing and laser cutting a music box

Step 1
  • Design a box
    • For the design to make it not just a regular box, there are hinges to allow rotation of the lid

    Step 2

  • Find a image that you want to engrave
    • This is an extra step and is not necessary to the function of the box and only for looks

    Step 3

  • Save design as a dxf and put into coreldraw or inkscape

    • This is to check whether the design transfered over properly and where you can start to print.
    You can also put the images here to engrave it onto the box.

    Step 4

  • Set the Machine laser settings and wait for the box to be cut out

    • Step 5

  • Test assemble the box to see if the parts fit and adjust design to cut again if needed

    • Step 6

  • Drill hole for lever to go through

    • Step 7

  • Final Assembly

    • Improvements I could have done

  • Feedback for myself
    • 1. I could have measured the dimension more accurately to reduce its size and save material usage

    2. I could have removed the background of my engraving image as even white background can cause bugs

    3. I could have engraved more to make the box look nicer

    4. I could have went with a better design as the roof is quite simplisic and boring
